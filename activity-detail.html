Description

Ziarah is an Arabic word that means "visit" and is used to describe a type of tour showcasing some of the significant locations linked to the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and his companions.

Makkah is known as the holiest city in Islam and represents the birthplace of Prophet Muhammad and therefore Islam itself.

Visit the symbolic sites in Makkah with our experienced Ziarah guides.
